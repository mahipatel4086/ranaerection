About Rana Erection Company

Rana Erection Company is a reliable and experienced company specializing in industrial erection and fabrication work. We provide high-quality structural erection, steel fabrication, and industrial support services for various projects.

With skilled manpower and strong technical knowledge, we ensure every project is completed safely, efficiently, and on time. Our company is committed to delivering durable work, maintaining safety standards, and building long-term relationships with our clients.

Our services include structural steel erection, industrial fabrication, heavy equipment installation, plant maintenance, and piping support work. We have successfully completed projects across various industrial sectors, earning the trust of our clients.
